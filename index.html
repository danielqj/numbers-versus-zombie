How to Play:

  • Zombies appear from the left with math problems on their heads
  • Solve the problem correctly to destroy the zombie
  • Wrong answers make zombies move closer to your fortress
  • Get 5 consecutive correct answers = 5 bonus points
  • Boss stage: Destroy the boss by answering 5 questions correctly in a row
  • Reach 100 points to win!
  • Difficulty increases as you progress - zombies get faster!
